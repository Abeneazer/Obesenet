About Us

Welcome to Discover Ethiopia

We believe that travel changes your perspective, but planning it shouldn't change your sanity.

This site was born out of a passion for exploring the world and a frustration with complicated, generic travel advice. We wanted to create a space that skips the tourist traps and cuts straight to what actually makes a trip unforgettable.

Our Mission

To promote authentic travel experiences in Ethiopia — from the ancient rock churches of Lalibela to the majestic castles of Gondar and the vibrant culture of Harar.

Our Location

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

We are based in the capital city of Ethiopia, allowing us to provide the most accurate and up-to-date local information for your journey.
